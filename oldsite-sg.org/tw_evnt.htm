Places of interest
Kanapaha Botanical Gardens
    Monday, Tuesday, Friday, 9am - 5pm
    Wednesday, Saturday, Sunday, 9am - dusk
    Closed Thursday
    This 62-acre botanical garden is environmentally diverse, offering mature hardwood forests interspersed with rolling meadows and magnificent vistas overlooking Lake Kanapaha. Ten major gardens are connected by a mile-long loop of paved walkways, making virtually the entire facility wheelchair accessible. Approximately 80% of the walkway traverses shaded woodlands, a feature that makes the jaunt comfortable during the oppresively hot days of late summer when floral color is at its peak. Off Archer Road, 1 mile west of I-75, Gainesville, (352) 372-4981.

    Admission

    • $3 for adults
    • $2 for children 6 to 13 years of age
    • free for children under 6 years of age
    • free for members of the North Florida Botanical Society

    Find out how you can honor a person or organization you care about and help build the new Summer House at Kanapaha Botanical Gardens.
