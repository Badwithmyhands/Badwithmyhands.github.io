We're Expecting! Agave Babies are on the Way!

SouthernGardening.org and Notestein's Nursery are proud to announce the immanent arrival of hybrid Agave babies, due in the Summer of 2015.

Jim Notestein with "Mom"

"Dad" (on loan from Grandiflora Nursery)

With help from a generous pollen donor, one of our in-house Agaves of unknown provenance is now "in a family way" and we expect it to produce viable seed of desirable new cultivars within the next few months.

Many thanks to Alan Shapiro of Grandiflora Nursery, Gainesville, FL, for the loan of a flowering Agave from amongst his stock.
