Help! We Need Your Agave Pollen!

Sometime in January or February, 2015, one of our Agaves--of unknown provenance--is expected to bloom. We would like to cross-pollinate it to produce a unique and (we hope) desirable cultivar to share with the plant community.

Sadly, no other of our hundreds of Agaves are blooming simultaneously, so we need your help!  If you have an Agave in bloom at this time, we would be thrilled if you could send us some pollen, along with any information you can provide about the source and some photos of the plant from which the pollen was obtained.

Please send it in a waterproof wrapping to:
